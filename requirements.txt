## 📝 How to Update on GitHub

### **Method 1: Web Interface (Easiest)**

1. **Go to your repository**
2. **Click on `requirements.txt`**
3. **Click the ✏️ pencil icon** (top right)
4. **Select ALL content** (Ctrl+A / Cmd+A)
5. **Delete it**
6. **Paste the content above**
7. **Scroll down**
8. **Commit message**: `Fix: Update to working dependency versions`
9. **Click "Commit changes"**

### **Method 2: Download and Upload**

1. **Download the file** I provided above
2. **Go to your repository**
3. **Click on `requirements.txt`**
4. **Click the trash icon** to delete it
5. **Click "Commit changes"**
6. **Click "Add file" → "Upload files"**
7. **Upload the downloaded file**
8. **Commit**
